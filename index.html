Safety concerns There are safety concerns related to the improper use of drones. All drone operators should be aware of the rules and responsibilities that apply to them before flying. Drone rules that apply to recreational users can be found on the CASA website. If someone has been seriously injured or needs emergency assistance please dial 000 (Emergency Services) immediately. Community members who witness unsafe drone behaviour are encouraged to report this via CASA's website. This form should be used when you think drone operations might have broken the safety rules. Safety breaches can only be investigated where there is sufficient evidence, such as photos or video recordings of the breach and the person controlling the drone at the time. Any evidence you can capture at the time of the incident will help support the investigation process.
